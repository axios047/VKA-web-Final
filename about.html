About Us

VitalSource Knowledge Associates Private Limited [VKA] is a management consultancy company with its registered office in Delhi NCR. We provide quality management and business consultancy services to national and international clientele in the knowledge process outsourcing space, especially in select finance verticals.

Values & Culture


We believe in serving our clients conscientiously and strive to provide them value through professionalism and integrity. As a solution provider in accounting, finance, HR, organization and management matters we position our services to ensure that our clients make distinctive, significant and lasting improvements in their performance. We help in building businesses that are efficiently system and process oriented rather than person oriented. Hence our focus is on maintaining a high quality and standard for services rendered and provide tailored business solutions for meeting and exceeding client’s expectations.

Corporate Social Responsibility


We believe that at India’s current stage of economic growth, corporates should play a crucial role in the country’s development by supporting government and international initiatives in areas like employment generation, poverty alleviation, environment conservation, social uplift etc. Accordingly VKA will position its services in Corporate Social Responsibility areas to help businesses carry out their operations ethically and contribute to India’s economic development by improving the quality of life of the workforce and their families as well as the community at large. We aspire to evolve services that take account of latest developments in this area that assist client companies to take initiatives and explore business opportunities that are socially responsible and contribute to projects that benefit all stakeholders of a Company including shareholders, employees, consumers, suppliers, the community and the government.
